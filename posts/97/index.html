"Immortality" is a single from Céline Dion's <Let's Talk About Love> album. It was released on June 8, 1998 with the Bee Gees on background vocals.

"Immortality" was composed by brothers Barry Gibb, Robin Gibb and Maurice Gibb, members of the Bee Gees, specially for Canadian singer Céline Dion.

There are two music videos. First one, directed by Scott Floyd Lochmus, shows Céline Dion and the Bee Gees in the recording studio (1997). Second one was directed by Randee St. Nicholas and released at the end of July 1998.

The song was a commercial success reaching #1 in Brazil (the Cuca mixes). It was #2 in Austria and Germany, #4 in Europe and #5 in the United Kingdom. However, the song was never released as a single in the United States.

"Immortality" was certified platinum in Germany (for over 500,000 copies sold), and silver in France (145,000) and the UK (200,000). Live version of this song was included on the One Night Only CD and DVD by the Bee Gees, released on November 3, 1998. Céline Dion performed this song also during her Let's Talk About Love Tour.

"Immortality" was a part of Dion's 1999 greatest hits album All the Way… A Decade of Song.

Celine Dion和她的专辑《Let's Talk About Love》
Celine.Dion 1968年3月30日生于加拿大魁北克省 Montreal 市的 Charlemagne。早在14岁时她已在当地非常出名。她的双亲以及家族拥有一支乐队并且到处演奏民族的音乐，这对她的影响很深。Dion 的母亲在她十二岁时为她写了第一支歌，她和她的哥哥共同录制了这首歌。之后她的母亲把这对兄妹带到 Rene Angelil 的办公室，这位当地的摇滚乐制作人成为了这位小童星走向辉煌的领路人。1990年才学了一年英语的 Dion 发行了她首张英语专辑《Unison》。虽然这张专辑为她带来4首经典单曲，但真正在国际上获得成功的是她为迪斯尼电影《美女与野兽》演唱的原声音乐。她首度与男歌星 Peabo.Bryson 合作的影片标题曲登上美国排行榜第一名，并且为她赢会一座奥斯卡最佳电影音乐奖和一座格莱美音乐奖。

1997年她发行了专辑《Let's Talk About Love》，一首为电影《Titanic(泰塔尼克号)》演唱的主题曲《My Heart Will Go On》再次轰动全球。她与 'Bee Gees' 乐队合作的《Immortality》以及与 R.Kelly 合作的《I'm Your Angel》也成为世界范围内的金曲。BeeGees 甚至还为《Immortality》这首美妙的, 能感动许多人的心的歌唱和音部分。这首歌是由Walter A. 在纽约城里监制的。
